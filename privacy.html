StyleGrab — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-19
The short version: StyleGrab collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, no server of ours in the loop at all.

What StyleGrab does

StyleGrab is a Chrome extension that lets you hover an element on a webpage, see its CSS, and copy it in a chosen format (CSS, Tailwind, JSX, styled-components, Vue, or Svelte). All extraction, formatting, and rendering happens entirely inside your browser, on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. StyleGrab does not operate any backend server, does not use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs, and does not track your browsing. We have no way to see what pages you use it on, what CSS you extract, or what you do with it.

The one third party: license validation

StyleGrab's Pro tier is unlocked with a license key purchased through Dodo Payments, our merchant-of-record payment provider. When you activate a key on the Options page, the extension sends that key to Dodo's public license-validation API to confirm it's real. Dodo's own privacy policy governs that exchange — we never see your payment details; Dodo handles checkout entirely.

A stored key silently re-validates itself every few days so a purchase doesn't need re-entering. If you're offline, StyleGrab keeps your existing Pro status rather than locking you out.

Local storage

Your settings and license state live in chrome.storage, tied to your Chrome profile. Uninstalling the extension removes it. We never receive a copy.

Changes to this policy

If this ever changes, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect it. Given the extension's zero-backend architecture, we don't expect that to happen often.
